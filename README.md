# TheCorrupted
Disclaimer
All Art is recolored or generated with Gemini (AI). I am sadly not capable of drawing them myself, maybe i will look into it after the mod is completed. If you want to make art for this mod feel free to do so!

Description
This mod adds a new Character called The Corrupted (Inspired by The Cursed Mod from Slay the Spire https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1670942227).
Currently the mod is being developed actively and the goal is to have a custom card pool, potion pool and relic pool. For Now the Character does not have an animation, i will look into it, after everything else is finished.

Installation instructions
1. Download the mod.
2. Unzip the mod. Keep .dll, .json and .pck files in one folder.
3. Drag and drop the created folder into Slay the Spire 2/mods folder.

Main features
Ritual: When playing a card with Ritual, you have to exhaust a card, if a Curse card is exhausted, you gain the Ritual effect.
Doomed: Card can be played normally, or if you end your turn with a Doomed card in your hand, it gets played, but with a lower effect and it adds Doom to yourself.
Cursed: Doomed: Card can be played normally, or if you end your turn with a Doomed card in your hand, it gets played, but with a lower effect and
it adds Random Curse cards to your deck.

You also gain other effects around your Curses and/or Doom stacks on yourself.
More is planned:

- Maybe add another mechanic other than only curses and/or Doom stacking yourself (Like Summoning or Forging)
